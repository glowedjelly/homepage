Reporting Guidelines

To help us resolve issues effectively, please follow these guidelines when reporting bugs or problems:

  • Search first: Check if your issue has already been reported before creating a new one.
  • Be descriptive: Provide a clear and concise summary of the problem in the title and description.
  • Steps to reproduce: List the steps needed to reproduce the issue.
  • Expected vs. actual behavior: Describe what you expected to happen and what actually occurred.
  • Include environment details: Mention your OS, browser, Node.js version, and any other relevant information.
  • Attach screenshots or logs: If possible, include screenshots or error logs to help us understand the issue.
  • Be respectful: Communicate politely and constructively.
Example:
Title: Button on homepage does not respond to clicks
Description: When I click the "Click me!" button on the homepage using Chrome on Windows 10, nothing happens. I expected a greeting to appear. Console shows no errors.

Thank you for helping us improve the project!
